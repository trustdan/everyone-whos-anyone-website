Rejoice that you're not down in the hole they're in...unless, of course, you are. Had we but world enough, and time, I'd stick in guys like Google, Microsoft, Apple, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and the umpteen other new uber-Nazis who own and operate what little's left of our mummified hearts and stupified minds. They have us laughing at cats on skateboards while we stand in line to vote. Pfssh. Politics is sillier than cats on skateboards. We talk and think and live and work the way our owners need us to talk and think and live and work in order to keep skinning us alive. Billions of sweet, loving souls eat shit their whole lives so the chosen few can live like kings. What to do about it? Let's turn out the lights and laugh at more cats on more skateboards.
